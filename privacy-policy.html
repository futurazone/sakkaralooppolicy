Privacy Policy for Sakkara Loop

Effective Date: September 13, 2025

Futura Zone ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Sakkara Loop mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

Information You Provide to Us

  • Nickname: We collect a player nickname that you voluntarily provide. This nickname is used to identify you on the public leaderboards. You are not required to use your real name.

Information Collected Automatically

While using our Service, we automatically collect the following gameplay data:

  • Score and Level: We collect your final score and the level you reached in each game session to rank you on the leaderboards.
  • Country Code: We collect the region code (e.g., "US", "ES") from your device's localization settings. We use this information solely to display your country's flag and to provide country-specific leaderboards. We **do not** collect your precise GPS location.
  • Timestamp (`created_at`): Our database automatically records the date and time when a score is submitted. This is used for creating monthly and time-based leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

Futura Zone uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To display your score, nickname, and country flag on global, national, and time-based leaderboards.
  • To provide personalized advertising through our third-party partners.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service and prevent cheating.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide our Service, which may collect information about you. Below are the services we use and links to their privacy policies:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for our backend services, including database hosting for leaderboards and secure score submission functions. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising ID to provide personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings. Google Privacy Policy.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, such as hashing score submissions, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information (such as a nickname), please contact us at the email below. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.
