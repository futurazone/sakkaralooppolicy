Data Deletion Request for Sakkara Loop

At Futura Zone, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. The only personally identifiable information we store on our servers is the nickname you provide for the leaderboards, along with your associated game scores.

To request the deletion of your nickname and all associated scores from our leaderboards, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support address: futurazone+dev@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, please clearly state the exact player nickname you wish to have deleted.

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that we may need to ask for verification if the nickname is common, to ensure we are deleting the correct data.

Thank you for playing Sakkara Loop!
